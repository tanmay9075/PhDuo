## 📘 What is PhDuo?

PhDuo is a Windows application that helps you find the best PhD advisor match. It compares your CV with professor profiles to show how well you fit with potential advisors. This app uses advanced AI technology to analyze your experience and offer insights into research fit, mentoring style, and lab culture. 

PhDuo gives you detailed reports with scores, risks, and suggestions. It helps you improve your application strategy and focus on the best opportunities.

## ▶️ Running PhDuo for the First Time

1. Open PhDuo from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.
2. The main screen will prompt you to upload your CV. Supported formats are PDF and DOCX.
3. Upload your CV file from your computer.
4. PhDuo will then ask for the professor’s profile or website URL. The app will automatically crawl up to 10 pages of the professor’s website to gather information.
5. Wait while PhDuo processes the data. This may take a few minutes.
6. After analysis, the app will show you a detailed report that includes:
   - Compatibility score
   - Research alignment
   - Mentorship style fit
   - Risk factors and gap analysis
